How to cook Chicken Pho

Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Chicken
  • Dried Rice Noodles
  • Water
  • Spices
    • Cloves
    • Fennel
    • Anise
    • Coriander
    • Cinnamon
  • Toppings
    • Onion/Ginger
    • Fish Sauce
    • Sugar
    • Lime
    • Chilli
    • Coriander
    • Noodles
    • Beansprouts
    • Mint
    • Thai Basil

Instructions

  1. Char the onion and ginger in a pan
  2. Get a big pot - add 6 liters of water and boil it
  3. Add in Chicken and herbs and simmer for 1.5 hours
  4. Prepare the noodles before serving! You boil it for 5 minutes in boiling water and then set them in cold water for 10-20 seconds
  5. When the chicken and soup is fully cooked - Strain the chicken and serve it with all the toppings
  6. If you have anything left over you can freeze it and reuse it for next time
